Mission Statement

At 'Lakeside Timber Co.', our passion is turning your dreams into reality. Built by Hand, Designed for Living. Whether it’s transforming an outdoor space into an oasis, crafting a unique indoor environment, or designing bespoke furniture, we blend master craftsmanship with innovative design. Every project is a commitment to quality, functionality, and artistry—ensuring that no matter what you imagine, we can create a space you love.

Core Services

  1. Outdoor Living Spaces: Custom decks, patios, pergolas, outdoor kitchens, gazebos, and more.
  2. Transformations: Bespoke interior modifications, including feature walls, built-in cabinetry, and custom room enhancements.
  3. Custom Furniture: Unique, handcrafted furniture pieces tailored to your specific style and space.

Competitive Advantage

  1. Master Craftsmanship: Every project is personally overseen by our master carpenter, ensuring exceptional quality, precision, and meticulous attention to every detail.
  2. Comprehensive & Versatile Service Range: From indoor transformations and outdoor sanctuaries to custom-built furniture, our diverse offerings make us your one-stop partner for all your design needs.
  3. Personalized Design Journey: We collaborate closely with our clients to translate their visions into tailored, actionable plans that perfectly align with their lifestyle and budget.
  4. Premium Quality & Sustainability: Our commitment to sourcing high-quality, eco-friendly materials guarantees enduring durability and an elevated aesthetic that stands the test of time.
