About Us

At 'Lakeside Timber Co.', craftsmanship runs deep in our roots. Founded on a passion for fine woodworking and expert design, we are dedicated to creating custom-built spaces that seamlessly blend beauty, functionality, and longevity.

Meet the Maker - Shelley Caine

Born and raised on Vancouver Island, British Columbia, I've had a passion for woodworking for as long as I can remember. From a young age, I was drawn to the art of building—fascinated by the way raw materials could be transformed into something both functional and beautiful.

I began my career as a labourer and carpenter's helper in my late teens, working across various construction and millwork shops to gain hands-on experience. By the age of 24, I fully stepped into my role as a carpenter and have dedicated myself to honing my craft ever since. With years of experience in both structural and fine woodworking, I bring a deep understanding of materials, design, and technique to every project.

At 'Lakeside Timber Co.', I take pride in offering handcrafted, high-quality solutions that bring my client's visions to life—whether it’s a custom piece of furniture, a stunning outdoor living space, or a beautifully designed interior feature.

- Shelley Caine

The Business Side - Caitlynn O'Neil

With a background in real estate and design, Caitlynn brings a unique perspective to 'Lakeside Timber Co.' As a real estate professional with The O'Neil Group, she has spent years helping clients find their dream homes—and now, she's helping them transform those homes into spaces that truly reflect their style and needs.

Real estate has been in Caitlynn's blood since childhood. Her family has been in the industry for generations, and she's spent years learning how homes are not just investments but deeply personal spaces. Combining her eye for design with her knowledge of home value, Caitlynn ensures that every project we take on is not only visually stunning but also a smart investment for homeowners.

Together, we blend expert craftsmanship, thoughtful design, and a deep understanding of how people live in their spaces-creating one-of-a-kind, handcrafted environments that stand the test of time.

- Caitlynn O'Neil
